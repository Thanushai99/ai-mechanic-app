DashSignal AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

```
DashSignal AI provides visual dashboard-warning guidance only. It is not a mechanical diagnosis and should not be relied on in an emergency or when immediate vehicle safety is in question.

1. Overview

DashSignal AI is operated by Thanushai Kandiah Ariyanayagam. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the DashSignal AI mobile app.

2. Dashboard Photos and AI Analysis

When you choose or take a dashboard photo for analysis, the image is sent to our analysis service through Supabase and then processed using Google Gemini AI technology. The image is used to identify visible dashboard warning indicators and provide cautious next-step guidance.

We do not intentionally save dashboard photos as part of the app's scan-history feature. However, service providers may process submitted information according to their own applicable terms and privacy policies.

3. Scan History

DashSignal AI may save scan-result information locally on your device, including the date of a scan, warning-light result, severity, explanation, and recommended next steps. Dashboard photos are not saved in scan history.

You can delete saved scan history at any time from within the app by using the Clear All option.

4. Technical Information

The app creates an anonymous technical session to securely communicate with the analysis service. We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or phone number to use the core dashboard-analysis feature.

5. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to provide dashboard warning-light analysis, maintain the app's core functionality, improve reliability, and help protect the service from misuse.

6. Data Sharing

Dashboard photos submitted for analysis are shared with service providers needed to operate the feature, including Supabase and Google Gemini. We do not sell personal information.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable measures designed to protect information while it is transmitted and processed. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Children

DashSignal AI is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. The latest version will always be available at this page, with the updated date shown above.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: thanushai1999@gmail.com
